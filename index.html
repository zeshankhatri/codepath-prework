How to Play:

Close x

Normal Game:

Click "Start" to begin the game.

To begin, a button will light up and play a sound, followed by an extra button each round. Press the buttons in the pattern shown each round to win the game!

Time running out or an incorrect button press will count as a strike and the pattern will play again. Three strikes lead to game over.

unlimited Game:

Click "Play Unlimited" to begin the game.

Concept is similar to the normal game except there's an infinite number of rounds, with the game only ending after three strikes.

You will be notified about which round you reached when the game finishes, providing you with an opportunity to add you name to the leaderboard.

Either game can be quit anytime by clicking "Stop".
